SMOOV

SMOOV is an app made in 2014. It allows e-vehicle drivers to find charging points, save them and check their availability. The user experience and visual design were no longer sufficient for the digital age. I helped in improving the way the app helps users visually.

Redesigning the user flow

To make the added functionalities and the new user flow visual, I made an InVision prototype showcasing the new functionalities. Without the actual context, it might look intimidating at first:

Visual design

Parallel to creating a new user flow, SMOOV underwent a rebranding. New colors and a fresh, modern font were chosen and I was assigned the task to apply the new visual style to the revamped application.

Result

After I worked on SMOOV, the app underwent some internal iterations. The app is available for download in the Play Store and the App Store.
